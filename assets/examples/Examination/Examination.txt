Title:
Examination

Description:
The example illustrates the collaboration between Students, the Secretary and the Professor in order to perform an exam session.
The Students interacts with the Secretary to book the exam. In consequence the Secretary advise the Professor, once he close the
session, the examination starts. Finally Students ends their processes referring to the received mark.

Versions:
Examination.bpmn - Correct version
Examination_issue1.bpmn - Max Pool violation - The Secretary pool has Maximum wrongly set to 1, so that the firing of 5 instances by means of message Access violates the pool multi-instance parameters.
